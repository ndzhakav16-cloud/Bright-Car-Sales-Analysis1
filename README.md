# Bright-Car-Sales-Analysis1
Business Insights for a New Head of Sales using Historical Car Sales Data from Bright Motors 

# Problem Statement
- The business has been experiencingsales performance across different models, regions, and time periods.
- Management requires a clear understanding of what drives sales volume, revenue, and profitability and meaningful insights to support data-driven strategic planning.
-This analysis aims to transform the raw sales data into meaningful insights to support data-driven strategic planning.

# Aim
-To analyze Bright Cars’ processed sales dataset to identify trends, performance gaps, and opportunities for growth through data visualization, KPI tracking, and actionable insights.

 # Objectives
- To examine sales performance across car models, regions, and time periods.  
- To identify key drivers of sales revenue and profitability.  
- To analyze customer demand patterns and pricing trends.  
- To design interactive dashboards*and visualizations for decision-making.

  # Summary of Report
* Insights Discovered:
- Top-performing models contributed over 60% of total revenue.  
- Seasonal peaks in sales observed between May–August, aligning with marketing campaigns.  
- SUVs generated higher profit margins compared to small hatchbacks.  
- Sales were strongest in urban regions, with underperformance in rural branches.  

# Recommendations
-Focus Marketing on High-Margin Models: Prioritize promotions for SUVs and mid-range sedans that yield higher profits.  
- Regional Targeting: Increase investment in underperforming rural regions through localized marketing campaigns.  
- Optimize Inventory Management: Align stock levels with seasonal demand and historical sales patterns.  
- Discount Optimization: Apply strategic discounting—use data to set optimal price points that boost volume without eroding profit.  
- Sales Team Performance Tracking: Implement KPI dashboards to monitor salesperson effectiveness and reward high performers.  
- Data-Driven Decision Culture: Continue leveraging analytics tools to make evidence-based business decisions.
